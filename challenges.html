Our application makes running fun and challenging. With different game modes such as the ghost or sightseeing run, your workout becomes more fun and energizing. Complete challenges for rewards, such as badges and items for your avatar. Follow the challenges in story mode, or choose a random challenge from our library. Feeling up for a challenge? Compete in ranked challenges, gain points, and see your name on global, national and local leaderboards. You can also challenge your friends to challenges and see who gets to them first...
Start Challenges Map View Goals & History Friends & Leaderboards

Complete the challenges in story mode to progress through the game and unlock new worlds. Gain XP and complete goals to earn rewards.

Not feeling the story right now? Choose a challenge from the library instead. Participate in seasonal challenges and challenge your friends.

There are many different challenges to choose from with great variation. Here as an example is the forest run, and one marathon in one week. The icons in the bottom right corner indicates whether a challenge has been completed, challenged by someone, or liked. All challenges can be completed in casual mode, and some are available as ranked challenges. When you complete a ranked challenge you earn points relative to how you performed vs. everyone else who also completed that challenge. This rating is what determines the leaderboard scores.
