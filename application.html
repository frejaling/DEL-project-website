Work together to save the world in this puzzle co-op game. Explore the dark world, find the puzzles, and solve the logic to bring back color and light.

Each player has their own set of abilities. One is tall and strong and can lift and drag objects, and the other is smaller and jump high and use her wand to light up objects to reveal secrets.

There are multiple levels, and each level has its own set of puzzles to solve before moving on to the next. You begin your adventure just outside the forest, to steadily make your way to the big city.
