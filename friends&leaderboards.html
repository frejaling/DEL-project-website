Our application makes running fun and challenging. With different game modes such as the ghost or sightseeing run, your workout becomes more fun and energizing. Complete challenges for rewards, such as badges and items for your avatar. Follow the challenges in story mode, or choose a random challenge from our library. Feeling up for a challenge? Compete in ranked challenges, gain points, and see your name on global, national and local leaderboards. You can also challenge your friends to challenges and see who gets to them first...

Send and receive challenges from friends to encourage eachother.

Complete ranked challenges and see how you did on the leaderboards. Leadersboards are global, national, and local, with support to add a leaderboard within your friend group.
